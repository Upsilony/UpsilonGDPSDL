Upsilon GDPS Demonlist Rules

This document contains every rule required for a level to be added to the Demonlist or for a record to be accepted. Make sure to carefully read all rules before submitting levels or records.

Record Submission Rules (Cross-Platform)

  • A full 0â€“100 playthrough with no cuts is required. (If your submitted video has cuts, you must include raw footage without cuts.)
  • A reasonable amount of previous attempts must be shown. A single death at 1% is not enough. (Everplay submissions are exempt.)
  • Endscreen stats must be fully visible for at least one frame.
  • If your mod menu supports it (e.g., Megahack v7), Cheat Indicator must be visible.
  • Clicks are required for any completion.
  • Only records from the allowed mods list will be accepted. Refer to the official mod spreadsheet.
  • You must complete the exact level listed on the site. Always verify the level ID.
  • No secret routes or bug routes.
  • No easy modes. Only records on the unmodified level are accepted.
  • The level must be played on Geometry Dash 2.2 or above.
  • Platformer mode completions are not accepted.
  • Noclip accuracy records are not accepted.
  • CBF is allowed. Physics bypass is NOT allowed. TPS bypass is allowed up to a maximum of 240.
  • Verification videos must be submitted as separate uploads. You may combine completion videos, but not verifications.
  • Once a level enters the Legacy List, new records will no longer be accepted.

    • Record Submission Rules (Mobile)

  • All cross-platform rules apply.
  • Visual taps are mandatory.
  • Audible taps are required (even if barely audible).

    • Level Rules

  • Platformer levels are not allowed. Must be classic mode.
  • The level must be at least 30 seconds long and must be rated Hard Demon or above.
  • No crash triggers allowed.
  • If a level uses a Random Trigger, the editor must include a clear explanation of how the randomization works.
  • Levels based entirely on RNG are not allowed.
